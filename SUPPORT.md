# Support

Legged Model Zoo is research software. Support is best-effort and no response
time, numerical result, platform compatibility, or fitness for a particular
purpose is guaranteed.

For reproducible usage questions or defects, open a GitHub issue and include:

- the exact command or example;
- the complete error identifier and message;
- MATLAB release, operating system, and licensed toolboxes;
- framework version from `lmz.util.Version.current()`;
- model/problem IDs and versions;
- whether MATLAB had a desktop/display;
- a minimal artifact or input only when you are authorized to share it; and
- the output of the relevant local test or static check.

Do not post credentials, license tokens, private datasets, unpublished owner
decisions, or sensitive security details. Follow `SECURITY.md` for suspected
vulnerabilities. Questions about redistribution require an explicit owner or
authorized-licensor decision; maintainers cannot infer permission from a
README, repository visibility, or prior local migration.

Current limitations and verified environments are recorded in
`docs/RELEASE_CANDIDATE_STATUS.md`, `docs/TEST_STATUS.md`, and
`docs/KNOWN_DIFFERENCES.md`.
