# Code of conduct

Legged Model Zoo contributors and users should be able to participate without
harassment or intimidation. Be respectful, specific, and patient in technical
discussion. Critique ideas and evidence, not people. Welcome questions across
experience levels, acknowledge uncertainty, and correct mistakes openly.

Unacceptable conduct includes harassment, discriminatory or sexualized
language, threats, doxxing, sustained disruption, deliberate misrepresentation
of evidence, and retaliation against someone who reports a concern.

Repository maintainers may edit or remove contributions, restrict
participation, or take other proportionate action when behavior harms the
community. When practical, explain the reason and provide a path to repair.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers through the
GitHub repository's maintainer contact channel. Do not put sensitive personal
information in a public issue. Reports will be handled as confidentially as
the available platform permits; no response-time guarantee is implied.

This project-specific policy applies in repository spaces and when someone is
representing the project in public. It does not replace the terms of the
hosting platform or applicable law.
